How to use the form:

This form is a safe and confidential space for members of the LGBTQ+ community to share their experiences, ideas, and suggestions with our club. Whether you've had a homophobic or transphobic encounter, are struggling to feel heard as an queer student, or just want to connect with others who understand what you're going through, we are here to listen and offer support. Please fill out the form below to share your thoughts and experiences with us, and know that you are not alone.
